CLAT 2026 Notification — Dates & Registration

CLAT topics

Overview Eligibility Paper pattern / Syllabus Notification Courses NLUs Results FAQ
  • The official notification for CLAT 2026 was released by the Consortium of National Law Universities (CNLU) on 7 July 2025.
  • Registration for the exam opened on 1 August 2025 and will close on 31 October 2025.
  • The CLAT 2026 exam is scheduled to be held on 7 December 2025 (Sunday) from 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm.
  • The test will be conducted in offline mode (pen-and-paper) across multiple centres in India, including major cities.
  • Candidates should download their admit card when available and keep track of updates via the official portal: consortiumofnlus.ac.in
