Meet our Acads Head

PM

Pranab K Mehta

Pranab Mehta is one of the senior most faculty with CLATutor.com and takes care of Academics and Technology execution in the company. Ready to face any challenge thrown his way, he started his career in the food business. He then spent more than a decade training students for various competitive entrance examinations such as CLAT/IIT JEE, CAT/GMAT/GRE/Bank PO. He achieved recognition and respect for his innovative teaching and his commitment to guiding his students to success. His motto is 'life is all about experimenting opportunities that lie beyond the corner'. Pranab is a rare combination of a sportsman with a keen business acumen and love for mathematics. He has the rare knack of making students fall in love with one of the most dreaded subjects in academics. His ability to change a student's fear of maths into a passion makes him stand out as a teacher of exceptional abilities.

Niraj Kumar

Niraj is also associated with CLATutor as faculty since 2007. He is the brain behind the company's brand. A man with tremendous knowledge and experience of academics and marketing, he is a well known name in the student community. Niraj has successfully forged institutional tie ups to help the company reach out to the student community. An avid lover of numbers, Arithmetic is his forte.
