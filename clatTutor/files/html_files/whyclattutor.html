What is CLAT, and why is coaching important?

CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) is a national-level entrance exam for admission to top law universities in India, such as NLU Delhi, NLSIU Bangalore, and others. Coaching is essential because it provides structured preparation, expert guidance, and access to resources like mock tests and study material, which are crucial for cracking the exam.
